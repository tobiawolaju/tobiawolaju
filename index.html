Projects↗

ATOM — 5’7 Autonomous Humanoid ↗

Full-size humanoid for research and real-world tasks. Focus on torque-based control, sensor fusion, and onboard autonomy.

  • Control: Whole-body torque control + MPC gait generator
  • Sensing: IMU, encoders, ToF, GPS (low-cost stack)
  • Software: ROS2 + custom C++ controllers + Moveit2 plugins
  • Powertrain: Frameless BLDC + harmonic drives
  • Outcome: Stable locomotion under hardware constraints
