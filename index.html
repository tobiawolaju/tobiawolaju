Bio↗

Tobiloba Awolaju is an Electrical and Electronics Engineering student at Adeleke University, passionate about developing impactful technologies at the intersection of the physical and digital worlds. His expertise spans Robotics, AI, and Systems Engineering.

Education: EEE @ Adeleke University | CS50 @ Harvard (online)

Tobi designs and builds autonomous systems, focusing on areas like sensor-AI integration, control systems, and real-time OS security. He aims to contribute to innovative engineering solutions that solve real-world problems.

In his free time, Tobi works on DApps and experimental software/hardware projects, either for research or to generate additional revenue to fund his major engineering goals.
